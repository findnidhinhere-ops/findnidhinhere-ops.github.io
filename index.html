About Me

I am a Counseling Psychologist based in Kerala, India, holding a BSc in Psychology, MSc in Applied Psychology, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Counseling.

My work is rooted in empathy, emotional safety, and unconditional positive regard. I provide a non-judgmental space where individuals can explore anxiety, trauma, relationships, identity, and personal growth.
